# stories/rome/SPEC.md — Roman Holiday

A book in the shelf. This file owns Rome's story and look; it defers to master `FILES.md`
for storage and to `CLAUDE.md` for the workflow. The cast is shared (see `/characters`).

## Premise
A chaotic, funny day as the three siblings tour Rome — based on the poem "Three Siblings
Loose in Rome." Tone: warm, slapstick, affectionate.

## Format
- 8 panels, **panel-per-image** (each panel a full painted scene).
- Text (captions, bubbles, SFX) is a separate overlay layer, not baked into the art.
- Panel content/casting/bubbles are defined in `pages.json`.

## The 8 beats
1. Arrival — airport, "Where's the PIZZA?!"
2. Colosseum — Pepe's gladiator face-plant
3. Trevi Fountain — Pepe tosses his wallet
4. Spanish Steps — six-scoop gelato meltdown + pigeon
5. Pantheon — rain through the roof
6. Mouth of Truth — Pepe's fake bite scream
7. Vatican — necks craned at the ceiling
8. Bedtime — "Best trip ever… don't tell."

## Look
- Soft hand-painted anime / gentle watercolor (house style), warm Roman palette
  (terracotta, ochre, peach sky). Consistent with the locked cast references.

## Cover
