# stories/hogwarts/SPEC.md — The Shadow Over Hogwarts

Placeholder book (status: coming-soon). Story text exists as the poem "Three Siblings and
the Shadow Over Hogwarts"; panels not yet built. Cast shared (`/characters`).

## Premise
The siblings are pulled into a cursed magical castle and team up with three young wizards
to survive a monster gauntlet and lift the curse. Tone: spooky-but-silly adventure.

## Likely beats (from the poem)
Arrival via an old book → Devil's Snare → a troll → Dementors (Luna's surprise Patronus
fox) → giant spider → basilisk finale (eyes shut, teamwork) → feast → home.

## Look
House style (soft hand-painted anime), darker/moodier castle palette vs Rome's warmth.

## IP NOTE (important, decide before art)
The poem features Harry, Ron, and Hermione by name. Original fan-fiction *text* is fine,
but their **visual character designs are trademarked** and must NOT be reproduced as art.
For the comic: center our three siblings and use **generic original wizard friends**, or
handle the cameo without depicting the trademarked likenesses. Lock this in SPEC before
generating panels.
